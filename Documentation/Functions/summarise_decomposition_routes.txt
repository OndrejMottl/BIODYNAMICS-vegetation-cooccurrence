Summarize Decomposition Diagnostic Routes

Description:

     Computes fold-level shares and route-level summaries from variant
     metrics produced by `run_decomposition_route_cv()`.

Usage:

     summarise_decomposition_routes(variant_metrics = NULL)
     
Arguments:

variant_metrics: Data frame with route, fold, variant, status, and loss
          columns.

Value:

     Named list with `data_fold_shares` and `data_route_summary`.
