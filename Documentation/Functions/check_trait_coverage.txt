Check Trait Coverage Against Community Taxa

Description:

     Compares a character vector of taxon names from the community data
     against the taxa present in a wide-format trait table. Reports the
     proportion of community taxa covered by trait data and identifies
     which taxa are missing or extra.

Usage:

     check_trait_coverage(vec_community_taxa, data_trait_table)
     
Arguments:

vec_community_taxa: A character vector of unique taxon names from the
          community data.

data_trait_table: A wide-format trait table as returned by
          [make_trait_table()], with a `taxon_name` column.

Details:

     A summary of coverage statistics is printed to the console via
     `cli::cli_inform()`.

Value:

     A named list with the following elements:

     n_community_taxa Total number of unique taxa in the community
          vector.

     n_covered Number of community taxa present in the trait table.

     pct_covered Coverage as a percentage, rounded to one decimal
          place.

     vec_missing_taxa Character vector of community taxa absent from
          the trait table.

     vec_extra_taxa Character vector of taxa in the trait table not
          found in the community.

See Also:

     [make_trait_table()]
