Make Community Record Signatures

Description:

     Builds deterministic composition signatures for community records.

Usage:

     make_community_record_signatures(data_source = NULL)
     
Arguments:

data_source: A long-format community data frame with `dataset_name`,
          `sample_name`, `age`, `taxon`, and `pollen_count` columns.

Value:

     A tibble with one row per `(dataset_name, sample_name, age)`
     record and a sorted `community_signature` column.
