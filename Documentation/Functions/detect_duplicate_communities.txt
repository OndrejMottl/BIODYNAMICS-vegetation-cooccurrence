Detect Duplicate Modern Communities

Description:

     Detects modern sample records with identical sorted community
     composition.

Usage:

     detect_duplicate_communities(data_source = NULL)
     
Arguments:

data_source: A long-format community data frame with `dataset_name`,
          `sample_name`, `age`, `taxon`, and `pollen_count` columns.

Value:

     A tibble with one row per record involved in a duplicated
     community signature. Returns a zero-row tibble when no duplicate
     communities are detected.
