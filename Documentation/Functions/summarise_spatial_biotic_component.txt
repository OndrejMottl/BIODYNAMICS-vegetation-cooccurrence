Summarise Spatial Biotic Component

Description:

     Summarises the Associations component for spatial variance spread
     figures.

Usage:

     summarise_spatial_biotic_component(data_plot)
     
Arguments:

data_plot: Data frame returned by
          [prepare_spatial_variance_plot_data()].

Value:

     A tibble with median and 95 percent interval columns for the
     biotic co-occurrence component.
