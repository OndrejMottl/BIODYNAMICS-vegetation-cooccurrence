Summarize Predictive Decomposition Shares

Description:

     Summarizes fold-level predictive decomposition shares with median
     and 95 percent intervals.

Usage:

     summarise_predictive_decomposition(data_shares)
     
Arguments:

data_shares: Data frame returned by
          `compute_predictive_decomposition_shares()`. Must contain
          `component`, `share`, and `defined` columns.

Value:

     A tibble with one row per component.
