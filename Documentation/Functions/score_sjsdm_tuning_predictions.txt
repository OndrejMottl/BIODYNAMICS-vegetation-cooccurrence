Score sjSDM Tuning Predictions

Description:

     Calculates compact held-out binomial tuning metrics from aligned
     observed and predicted matrices.

Usage:

     score_sjsdm_tuning_predictions(
       data_observed = NULL,
       data_predicted = NULL,
       epsilon = 1e-06
     )

Arguments:

data_observed: Binary numeric matrix of held-out observations.

data_predicted: Numeric matrix of held-out probabilities with matching
          dimensions and dimnames. Missing prediction dimnames inherit
          observation dimnames.

 epsilon: Probability clipping tolerance strictly between zero and
          `0.5`.

Value:

     One-row tibble with retained taxa, response-value count, total and
     per-response negative log likelihood, and macro AUC.
